流金歲月 Sansui Collection

Collection of gems and precious ores

Collection of gems and precious ores is my personal pursuit, which becomes copious when time has passed. The collectables of historical values elevate not only personal discernment and tastes but also sculpt temperament. My antique treasures are made known to the keen eyes for the homogenous fondness now. The finest is orchestrated and encapsulated in this book entitled “The Arts of Jade Carving”, “Magnificent Archaic Bronzes” and “Masterpieces of Chinese Precious Metalwork” expressing my love towards homeland. This is also my honour to have Professor Qi Dongfang from the Peking University and others to write its preface. It is wished that people with the same fondness will find delight in it. I am well informed that you are affectionate of history, having a profound understanding in traditional Chinese cultures; therefore, I hereby present my book for your perusal, and be honoured to have your valuable feedback.
Yours sincerely
Sam Lam
